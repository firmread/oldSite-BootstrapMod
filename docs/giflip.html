That they is a two player iPad game about relationship that encourages physical collaboration and negotiation. As each player have to control and solve their character's problem in saperated world, they also have to physically negotiate on how to tilt iPad to control a power particle to let them both progress further within the game.

The project is written in OpenFrameworks (C++).
