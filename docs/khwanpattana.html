Khwanpattana is a local market in highly populated area in Bangkok, Thailand. This project is an reaction to the question of local/global for the Association of Siamese Architect annual competition on 2009. It propose a futuristic architecture that also serves as urban public space. Bringing elements such as recycling process and greenery into architectural elements to create awareness and sense of common responsibities on environmental effects.

Exhibited in Association of Siamese Architect Expo 2009
